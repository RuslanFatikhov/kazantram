What is this scheme?

In the summer of 2022, I started reading a book authored by Ilya Birman "Design of transport schemes". While reading, as an independent work, I decided to make a sheme of Kazan trams. I started planning the scheme of Kazan trams using the design and principles described in the book. I studied the rules and recommendations for the construction of such schemes and began to plan a tram map. First I looked at the map of the city and analyzed the routes. Then I drew a prototype of the scheme and constantly made changes, checking and studying the map of the city. Gradually, I added details to the scheme in the form of points of interest, subway crossings. After much preparation, I finally completed the scheme of Kazan trams. The result exceeded all my expectations
